A partner-facing brochure for Surfsight's data plan offering, designed for fleet operators and channel partners evaluating video telematics connectivity options.

The core challenge: data plans for edge AI cameras aren't inherently exciting. The work was to connect the technical specs (data throughput, latency thresholds, onboard processing vs. cloud upload trade-offs) to the outcomes fleet operators actually care about — uptime, compliance, insurance, driver coaching.

**What this piece demonstrates:**
- Technical product translated into channel/partner language
- Hardware-plus-software product positioning
- B2B brochure writing for a non-technical buyer persona
- Surfsight brand voice at the time of the Lytx acquisition
