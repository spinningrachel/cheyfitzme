A technical proposal written for a unique strategic partner, covering Coro's modular cybersecurity platform in the context of MSP onboarding for EDR and Endpoint Security.

The challenge: the partner's sales team needed to understand not just what Coro's platform did, but how to position it against incumbent point solutions their customers already owned. The proposal had to be technically credible without being a feature dump — it needed to map Coro's architecture to the partner's customer pain points and procurement process.

**What this piece demonstrates:**
- Technical writing for a non-technical sales audience
- Competitive framing without disparaging incumbents
- Modular product architecture explained for channel partners
- Compliance-adjacent positioning (cyber insurance, regulatory exposure as buying trigger)
