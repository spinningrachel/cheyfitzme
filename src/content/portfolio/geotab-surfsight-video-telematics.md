A co-marketing brochure produced for the Geotab and Surfsight partnership, targeting fleet managers and operations leaders evaluating an integrated GPS-plus-video telematics solution.

Co-marketing pieces are structurally harder than single-brand content — you're writing to serve two distinct brand voices, two sales teams, and two sets of customer priorities, without creating something so generic it serves neither. This piece had to make the integration feel inevitable rather than bolted on.

**What this piece demonstrates:**
- Co-marketing writing across two partner brands
- Fleet technology domain: GPS telematics + edge ML video
- Persona writing for fleet operations and logistics buyers
- Integration positioning (1+1 = 3 argument without saying it)
