## Page: Amplify Your Message — Positioning & Messaging

URL: `/core-positioning/`

### Intro
> "Is your software business struggling to communicate a unified message? Are you looking for a way to streamline your marketing efforts and ensure everyone's on the same page? Look no further. A strong positioning framework is your secret weapon for marketing success."

### Why a Positioning Framework Matters

**For Executive Leadership**
- Uniform Messaging: Ensure consistent communication across the entire business
- Smooth Onboarding: Help new leaders quickly align with core positioning
- Strategic Alignment: Keep messaging in sync with evolving company goals

**For Product Management**
- Strategy Communication: Easily share product strategy across the organization
- Roadmap Alignment: Support decision-making and keep teams informed
- Release Management: Ensure sensitive releases are communicated accurately

**For Marketing & Growth Leaders**
- Strategy Design: Influence and shape marketing strategies with confidence
- Execution Support: Plan and execute campaigns with precision
- Analyst Relations: Effectively communicate with industry analysts

**For Your Brand**
- Awareness and recognition: Consistency leads to faster brand recognition overall
- Consistency: Create content that accurately reflects your brand and product
- Efficiency: Save time by referencing a single source of truth

**For Your Business**
- Onboarding: Help new team members ramp up quickly
- Employee retainment: Uniformity supports success and satisfaction of every employee
- Accelerated growth & reduced Sales costs: awareness simplifies Sales processes

### Core Components of a Positioning Framework
- Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
- Ideal Partner Profile (IPP)
- Personas
- Relevant Verticals
- Company Trust: Certifications, Awards, Background and Expertise
- Brand Overview
- Product Name and Category
- Target Customer Segmentation
- Compelling Reasons to Buy
- Competitive Alternatives
- Differentiated Features
- Value Proposition
- Messaging Guidance and Guidelines

### Positioning Services
- ICP/IPP research and formation
- Persona profiles
- Market research, market gaps and market needs analysis
- Competitive analysis
- Messaging recommendations
- Positioning and messaging documentation
- Positioning and messaging templates
